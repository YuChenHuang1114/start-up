    About Us

    Step into our French bakery, where the essence of Paris mingles with the comforting flavors of Taiwan. Founded by a Taiwanese baker who found solace in the art of French baking while studying in France, our bakery is a heartfelt homage to the flavors of home. Despite being miles away, our founder's longing for Taiwanese warmth and hospitality infuses every creation. Each morning, our team lovingly prepares artisanal breads, pastries, and croissants, blending traditional French techniques with cherished Taiwanese ingredients. From crispy baguettes to indulgent éclairs, every bite carries the spirit of home and a touch of Parisian elegance. Join us on a journey that celebrates the fusion of cultures and the joy of finding home wherever you are, at our bakery.
